/02

Containment
Protocol

A three-stage process that runs entirely inside your browser. No servers, no telemetry, no data leaves your machine. Installed in ten seconds. Active on every tab.

01
Stage 01 · Detect

Scan the page

As soon as a page starts loading, Spoiler Shield scans text and images for score patterns (3-1, 2:0), outcome words (beat, thrash, won), and team names in 15+ languages.

→
02
Stage 02 · Redact

Hide the spoilers

Scores become shield icons. Outcome words are masked. Thumbnails near sports content are blurred. Happens before the page finishes rendering — no flash of forbidden data.

→
03
Stage 03 · Reveal

On your terms

Watched the match? Press Alt + S to toggle off. Or click any individual element to reveal just that one. Full control, always.

Interrogation
Transcript

Answers to questions we've fielded. If yours isn't here, send it in.

Q · 01 Does this really work on every website? +
Yes. The extension runs on every URL you visit and scans page text and images for score patterns and sports keywords. We've tested it on YouTube, Google, Reddit, Twitter, ESPN, BBC, and many more. If you find a site where it misses a spoiler, email us and we'll patch it.
Q · 02 What languages are supported? +
Team names are recognized in English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Thai, Arabic, Turkish, Russian, Dutch, and more — 15+ languages total. The numeric score patterns (3-1, 2:0) are language-agnostic.
Q · 03 Do you collect any data? +
No. Nothing. All detection runs locally in your browser. No analytics, no tracking, no browsing history sent anywhere. You can read the privacy policy or inspect the source.
Q · 04 Why does it need access to all websites? +
Because spoilers come from everywhere. The extension needs permission to scan page contents to blur the spoilers. It only runs detection locally — nothing is transmitted.
Q · 05 Is it really a one-time payment? +
Yes. $4.99 once, yours forever, including all future updates. If we ever introduce an advanced subscription tier, existing one-time buyers keep everything they paid for. No rug-pulls.
Q · 06 What happens after the 14-day trial? +
Protection pauses. You'll see a clear prompt to upgrade for $4.99. You can ignore it, or uninstall — we won't nag. No credit card is stored during the trial.
Q · 07 Does it work on mobile? +
Not yet. Chrome on iOS and Android doesn't support extensions. Firefox Android is on the roadmap — subscribe to the blog for updates.
Q · 08 What if I find a spoiler that slips through? +
Email us with the URL. We patch detection rules fast — usually within the week — and ship updates automatically.
