# Privacy Policy — Spoiler Shield

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

## What Spoiler Shield Does

Spoiler Shield is a Chrome extension that hides sports scores and spoilers from websites you visit. It scans text on web pages locally in your browser and replaces spoiler content with shield icons.

## Data Collection

**Spoiler Shield does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No browsing history** is collected or sent anywhere.
- **No page content** leaves your browser. All text scanning happens locally on your device.
- **No analytics or tracking** of any kind.
- **No cookies** are set by the extension.
- **No third-party scripts** are loaded for tracking purposes.

## Data Stored Locally

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Enabled/disabled state** — whether the extension is turned on or off.
- **Install date** — stored in Chrome sync storage to manage your trial period. This date is tied to your Google account and is not accessible to us.
- **Payment status** — managed by ExtensionPay (Stripe). Payment is processed entirely through Stripe. We do not have access to your credit card details.

## Third-Party Services

- **ExtensionPay / Stripe** — used for payment processing only. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment transactions: https://stripe.com/privacy

## Permissions

- **Storage** — to save your on/off preference and trial status.
- **All URLs** — to scan and hide spoilers on any website you visit. No data is sent externally.
